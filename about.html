Our Story

Edlivky coding Academy is a programming company that aims to
radically transform the output of education in Africa through Tech education.
We have successfully reached over 100 youths and children in Nigeria preparing them for the future of work with the core skills
in programming and robotics. Our hands on, project-based and peer learning approach
is helping to build brilliant webs that will unleash globally competitive innovations from Africa.
